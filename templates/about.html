18 Years of Excellence

Nature Holidays - Your Trusted Travel Partner

Welcome to Nature Holidays, Wayanad's premier travel and tour agency with over 18 years of excellence in creating unforgettable travel experiences. Based in the heart of Kerala's most beautiful hill station, we have been the trusted choice for thousands of travelers seeking authentic, well-planned, and memorable journeys across India and beyond.

Our Story & Legacy

Founded in 2006, Nature Holidays began with a simple mission: to make travel accessible, enjoyable, and worry-free for everyone. What started as a small local travel agency in Wayanad has grown into one of the region's most trusted travel partners, serving clients from across India and international destinations. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, learning, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
