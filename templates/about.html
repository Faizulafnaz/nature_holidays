Nature Holidays — your travel partners from Wayanad

Welcome to Nature Holidays — your Wayanad travel partners with over 18 years of carefully planned holidays. From our base in Kerala's hills, we help families, couples, friends, and groups enjoy India and beyond with warmth, clarity, and peace of mind.

Our story

Founded in 2006, we began with a simple idea: travel should feel easy and human. What started as a small agency in Wayanad has grown through years of listening to travelers — shaping trips for college groups, honeymoons, family holidays, and quiet senior journeys across South India, North India, and international destinations.
